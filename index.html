Eloquent Souls: The 2nd Poetry Festival.

Greetings to everyone!!! *LITWITS- TINT Literary Club invites poetry submission from students of the college for "Eloquent Souls: The 2nd Poetry Festival."* Competent jury shall select outstanding poems to be read out by the respective poets in an offline event to be held on 17th January,2024. Teachers and staff members of the College are also invited to submit original poems that they intend to read at "Eloquent Souls" on 17th Jan. All submission must be original in Hindi, English or Bengali. Participation in elocution category is also invited where poems written by eminent poets can be read out. Last date for submission: 20th December 2023 Mail id for submission: literary.club@tict.edu.in

About TINT

Techno International New Town (formerly known as Techno India College of Technology) was established, with a vision to achieve national recognition as a premier technical institution, in 2005, and has since been providing quality education and research opportunities that fulfil the requirement of the industry, the academia, and the society.

Nandanik 2024

Join us at Nandanik 2024, where art and photography converge to ignite the senses! Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Nubra Valley and its hidden treasures in Sandipan Mukherjee's captivating photo essay, "On Broken Trails." Then, delve into the intriguing relationship between science and art with Mamata Mukherjee's thought-provoking session, "Science and Art: Contrasting, Yet Co-existing." Don't miss this unique opportunity to immerse yourself in creativity and inspiration at Nandanik 2024! TINT Art Club - Aesthetica TINT Photography Club
